Built for security professionals

Every tool you need to take a system from intake to Authority to Operate.

Multi-Region Intake Wizard

Select operating regions and the platform auto-determines applicable frameworks, adapts terminology, and surfaces region-specific compliance questions.

AI Evidence Guidance

Generate tailored evidence guidance per control. Upload documents for AI-assisted intake parsing. Elaboration engine helps clients write better evidence narratives.

ATO & iATO Generation

One-click PDF export of full ATO packages, interim ATOs with POA&M items, compliance breakdowns by control family, and risk categorization summaries.

Client & Team Portal

Invite clients to complete intakes and submit evidence. Assign peer assessors to projects. Full role-based access with owner/assessor/client scoping.

MFA, Passkeys & Audit Trail

Every sensitive action is MFA-signed (TOTP or WebAuthn biometric). Full audit log with cryptographic signature tokens for non-repudiation.

POA&M & Remediation

Track Plan of Action & Milestones with risk-based deadlines, status workflows, and auto-population from audit findings. Clients can remediate from their portal.

Dedicated tenant isolation

Every customer gets their own Azure App Service instance with an isolated SQLite database. No shared infrastructure, no cross-tenant risk. Your compliance data is physically separated at the compute and storage layer.

Automated provisioning deploys your instance in under 3 minutes. WAL-mode SQLite with daily encrypted backups to Azure Blob Storage ensures durability without the overhead of a managed database service.
